# Push / Pull / Legs (PPL) Routine (2x Weekly) - 2026 Science-Based

## Day 1: PUSH (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
*Focus: Stability for maximum mechanical load.*

* **Convergent Machine Chest Press**: 3 x 6-8 reps.
* **Smith Machine Incline Press**: 3 x 8-10 reps.
* **Cable Lateral Raises (Behind the back)**: 3 x 12-15 reps.
* **Pec Deck (Machine Flys)**: 2 x 10-12 reps.
* **Overhead Cable Triceps Extension (Katana Extension)**: 3 x 10-12 reps.
* **Triceps Pushdown (Rope or V-Bar)**: 2 x 12-15 reps.

---

## Day 2: PULL (Back, Rear Delts, Biceps)
*Focus: Vertical and horizontal pulling with chest support.*

* **Lat Pulldown (Neutral or Wide Grip)**: 3 x 8-10 reps.
* **Seated Cable Row (Chest-Supported if available)**: 3 x 10-12 reps.
* **Cable Pullover (Straight Bar or Rope)**: 2 x 12-15 reps.
* **Face Pulls or Machine Rear Delt Fly**: 3 x 15 reps.
* **Incline Dumbbell Curls**: 3 x 10-12 reps.
* **Cable Hammer Curls (Rope)**: 2 x 12-15 reps.

---

### Execution & Recovery Notes
* **Frequency**: Repeat the cycle (Days 1-2-3, Rest, Days 1-2-3).
* **Progressive Overload**: Aim to increase weight or add one extra rep every week.
* **Intensity (RPE 8-9)**: Stop the set when you feel you could have done at most 1 or 2 more reps with perfect form.
* **Rest Periods**: 2-3 minutes for compound exercises and 60-90 seconds for isolation exercises.
* **Eccentric Phase**: Control the descent of the weight (2-3 seconds) to maximize mechanical tension and muscle stretch (Stretch-Mediated Hypertrophy).
